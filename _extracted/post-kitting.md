# Kitting

At GatorTec, we know that setting up new Apple devices for your business, or organization can be time-consuming and complex. That’s why we offer kitting services, a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the deployment process for iPads, Macs, iPhones, and accessories. As an Apple Premier Partner, we ensure that your devices arrive fully prepared, pre-configured, and ready for immediate use, reducing downtime and IT workload.

Our kitting process goes beyond just packaging devices—we handle everything from device configuration and labeling to bundling necessary accessories such as power adapters, cases, keyboards, and Apple Pencils. Each kit is custom-built to meet your organization’s specific needs, ensuring that users receive a complete, organized package tailored to their workflows. Whether you’re deploying hundreds of iPads in a school district or rolling out new MacBooks for employees, GatorTec ensures that every device is prepped, protected, and efficiently packaged for easy distribution.

Kitting also enhances efficiency and consistency across large-scale deployments. By standardizing device setups, including pre-installed software, asset tagging, and enrollment into mobile device management (MDM) solutions, GatorTec helps IT teams simplify onboarding and maintain compliance with security policies. This means that users can unbox their devices and start working immediately, without requiring time-consuming manual setup or IT intervention.

With GatorTec’s kitting services, your organization gains a turnkey deployment solution that saves time, improves user experience, and ensures a smooth rollout of Apple devices. Whether you need devices ready for a corporate deployment, educational program, or retail solution, we provide a tailored approach that meets your unique requirements. Let us handle the details—so you can focus on what matters most. Contact us today to learn how our kitting services can simplify your next Apple deployment!
