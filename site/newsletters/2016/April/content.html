Staff's Pick of the Month
 
 
 
 
Each monthly newsletter spotlights a different GatorTec team member and showcases the technology they use in their everyday life. This month we’ll hear from Tyler, a GatorTec Sales Specialist.
 
 
 
 

 
As a full-time student and sales specialist at GatorTec, I’m constantly on the go. Few items help me more than my Macbook 12”, Brenthaven sleeve and PowerBeats.

Moving between classes, and to and from work, can get tiring and frustrating with heavy bags and electronics, but with my Macbook 12” in my Brenthaven Collins sleeve, traveling across campus is easy, compact, and light. The Brenthaven sleeve holds everything I need, with enough space for my Macbook, iPad mini, and chargers for everything. It looks great too, and offers plenty of drop protection.
 
 

Music is a necessity for me, so I constantly have music on in my car, at home, or when I’m studying. For those moments when I’m working out, or on the go, I love my PowerBeats 2 Wireless. They have great sound, with crisp bass and battery life that easily lasts all day. They’re indispensable at the gym, where cords from regular headphones can get tangled in equipment and damaged. Thanks to the PowerBeats, damage and tangles are never a problem.
