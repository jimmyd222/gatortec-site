AirTag

AirTag. Find your things in a flash.

AirTag is an easy way to keep track of your stuff. Attach it to your keys, slip it in your backpack, stick one in your luggage. Then use the Find My app to track it down, with audible alerts, on-screen guidance, and Precision Finding on supported iPhones.

Starting at $29

At a glance

Small. Smart. Privacy-first.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Find My network

    Hundreds of millions of Apple devices help you locate AirTag while keeping you anonymous through end-to-end encryption.

  • Precision Finding

    With a U1-equipped iPhone, see exact distance and direction to your AirTag with on-screen guidance.

  • Privacy-first design

    No location data or location history is physically stored inside AirTag. AirTags moving with someone else generate alerts on their iPhone.

  • User-replaceable battery

    A standard CR2032 coin-cell battery lasts about a year. Swap it yourself.

  • Free engraving

    Personalize each AirTag with emoji, letters, or numbers — free with purchase.

  • Splash, water, and dust resistant

    Rated IP67 (maximum depth of 1 meter up to 30 minutes).
