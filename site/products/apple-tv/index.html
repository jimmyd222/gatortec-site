Apple TV 4K

Apple TV 4K. The Apple experience, on your TV.

A19-class performance brings cinematic 4K HDR, Dolby Vision, and Dolby Atmos to your living room. The Apple TV app, Apple Fitness+, Arcade, plus a built-in HomeKit hub. Includes the refreshed Siri Remote.

At a glance

Key features

What makes it special.

  • 4K HDR + Dolby Vision

    Stunning picture quality with deep blacks, vivid color, and high contrast on supported TVs.

  • Dolby Atmos sound

    Cinematic, room-filling sound on supported speaker setups — or with AirPods using Spatial Audio.

  • Apple Fitness+ on the big screen

    Studio-style classes — strength, HIIT, yoga, dance, mindfulness — guided by world-class trainers.

  • Apple Arcade

    Access to a curated catalog of games on the biggest screen in your home.

  • Smart home hub

    Apple TV 4K acts as a HomeKit and Matter hub — control lights, locks, and accessories from anywhere.

  • Siri Remote

    Aluminum design, touch-enabled clickpad, dedicated Siri button, and USB-C charging. Find it with iPhone if you misplace it.
