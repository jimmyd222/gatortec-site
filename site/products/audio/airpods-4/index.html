AirPods 4

AirPods 4. Iconic. Reimagined.

A new acoustic architecture, a redesigned shape, and Personalized Spatial Audio — for a more immersive, more comfortable AirPods. Choose the standard model or add Active Noise Cancellation.

Starting at From $129

At a glance

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Redesigned for comfort

    A new contour shaped using millions of head and ear scans for a more secure, more comfortable fit.

  • Personalized Spatial Audio

    Use iPhone's TrueDepth camera to create a profile that adapts sound for your ears — built-in on both models.

  • Active Noise Cancellation option

    Step up to AirPods 4 with ANC for class-leading noise cancellation in an open-ear design — plus Transparency mode and Conversation Awareness.

  • Voice Isolation

    A new ML algorithm separates your voice from background noise during calls — clearer audio for you and the person on the other end.

  • USB-C charging case

    Smaller, lighter, and now charges over USB-C. ANC model adds wireless charging via Qi and Apple Watch chargers.

  • IP54 rated

    Dust, sweat, and water resistant. Designed to handle workouts, walks, and weather.
