AirPods Max. High fidelity. Five new colors.

A custom acoustic design, Adaptive EQ, and personalized Spatial Audio — for a deeply immersive sound. Now in five fresh colors, with USB-C and Lossless audio support.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Custom acoustic design

    Each ear cup houses a 40mm dynamic driver tuned for the broadest possible frequency response — from deep bass to crisp high notes.

  • Adaptive EQ

    Real-time tuning adjusts the sound to fit and seal of the ear cushions — and to what you're listening to.

  • USB-C charging

    A new USB-C port — same cable as your iPhone and Mac. Includes a high-quality USB-C cable for Lossless audio playback.

  • Personalized Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking

    Pinpoint accurate sound that follows your head — for movies, music, and FaceTime.

  • Memory foam ear cushions

    Knit-mesh canopy distributes weight to reduce on-head pressure. Memory foam cushions create an effective acoustic seal.

  • Smart Case

    Preserves battery with an ultralow power state when not in use.
