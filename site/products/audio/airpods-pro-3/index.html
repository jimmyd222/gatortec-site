AirPods Pro 3. The new standard.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Up to 2× the ANC

    A new H-series chip plus reengineered transducers and acoustic vents — Apple's most powerful noise cancellation ever.

  • Adaptive Audio

    Dynamically blends Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode to match your environment.

  • Heart rate sensing

    A photoplethysmography sensor measures heart rate during workouts — pair with Apple Fitness or supported apps.

  • Live Translation1

    Get spoken-language translation through AirPods Pro 3 with a paired iPhone — natural and hands-free.

  • Personalized Spatial Audio

    Dynamic head tracking and personalized HRTF make movies and music feel like they're happening around you.

  • IP57 dust, sweat, and water resistance

    Workouts, rain, dust — AirPods Pro 3 keep going. The case is rated IP57 too.
