HomePod mini

HomePod mini. Big sound. Five colors.

A computational audio engine packs surprisingly rich, 360-degree sound into a 3.3-inch sphere. Multiple HomePod mini speakers throughout your home let you play the same music everywhere — or different music in each room.

Starting at $99

At a glance

Mini in size. Huge in sound.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • 360-degree audio

    Acoustic waveguide directs sound down and out, filling the room in every direction.

  • Computational audio

    The Apple S5 chip applies complex tuning models in real time — analyzing every note 180 times per second.

  • Intercom

    Send a voice message to another HomePod, iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch anywhere in your home.

  • Smart home hub

    Built-in support for HomeKit and Matter — control your accessories from anywhere.

  • Hands-free Siri

    Music, timers, calls, the news, smart home control — all by voice.

  • Temperature and humidity sensing

    Trigger automations based on the conditions in your room.
