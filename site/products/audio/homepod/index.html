HomePod

HomePod. Room-filling sound.

A high-excursion woofer and a beamforming array of horn-loaded tweeters fill any room with deep, distortion-free sound. Spatial Audio support, stereo pair capability, and a built-in Siri smart hub for your home.

Starting at $299

At a glance

Sound that fills the room.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Room-sensing technology

    HomePod analyzes the acoustics of its location and tunes the sound automatically.

  • Spatial Audio

    Dolby Atmos and Spatial Audio tracks come alive with immersive, multi-dimensional sound.

  • Stereo pair

    Pair two HomePods of the same model for an even more expansive soundstage.

  • Smart home hub

    Built-in support for HomeKit and Matter — control lights, locks, thermostats, cameras, and more.

  • Hands-free Siri

    Far-field microphones hear you across the room. Music, timers, smart home, news — all hands-free.

  • Temperature and humidity sensor

    Trigger automations based on the conditions in your room — without extra hardware.
