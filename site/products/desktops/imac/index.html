iMac · M4 chip

iMac. Built for Apple Intelligence.

An all-in-one desktop, supercharged by the M4 chip. Browsing and multitasking is snappier than ever, with a stunning 24-inch 4.5K Retina display in an iconic design and an advanced camera, mics, and speakers.1

Starting at $1,299

Key features

Brilliant for work and play.

  • Fits perfectly in your space

    The all-in-one desktop design is strikingly thin, comes in seven vibrant colors, and elevates any space with style.

  • Supercharged by M4

    Get more done faster with the Apple M4 chip. From editing photos to creating presentations to gaming, you'll fly through work and play.

  • Built for AI

    Apple silicon is designed to run demanding on-device AI workloads like LLM inference and training. Apple Intelligence helps you write, express yourself, and get things done with groundbreaking privacy protections at every step.1

  • Immersive 4.5K Retina display2

    The 24-inch 4.5K Retina display features 500 nits of brightness and supports up to one billion colors. Nano-texture glass option (4-port models, Silver) reduces reflections and glare.

  • Advanced camera and audio

    Stay perfectly framed and sound great with a 12MP Center Stage camera, three studio-quality mics, and six speakers with Spatial Audio.

  • Connect it all

    Up to four Thunderbolt 4 ports for lightning-fast data transfers, support for up to two external 6K displays, plus Wi-Fi 6E3 and Bluetooth 5.3.
