Mac Studio · M4 Max and M3 Ultra chips

Mac Studio. More power to you.

A pro desktop with the phenomenal M4 Max or M3 Ultra chip. With massive memory, mind-bending graphics, next-level AI capabilities, and superfast Thunderbolt 5 ports, Mac Studio is a powerhouse in a compact and quiet design. Built for Apple Intelligence.1

Starting at $1,999

Key features

Pro performance, pro everything.

  • Supercharged by M4 Max or M3 Ultra

    Take on extreme workflows — detailed visual effects, 3D animation, film scoring. A powerful Neural Engine supports AI in complex tasks; advanced GPU architecture supports Dynamic Caching, mesh shading, and ray tracing.

  • Phenomenal memory and storage

    Up to 128GB of unified memory and 8TB of storage with M4 Max. Up to 256GB of unified memory and 16TB of storage with M3 Ultra.2

  • Built for AI

    Apple silicon is designed to run demanding on-device AI workloads like LLM inference and training. Apple Intelligence helps you get things done with privacy at every step.1

  • Fits right on your desk

    The compact 7.7-inch-square Mac Studio enclosure is designed to fit perfectly under most displays.

  • Runs cool and quiet

    The thermal system lets you fly through intensive tasks at incredible speeds while keeping Mac Studio quiet — it never interferes with your workflow.

  • Powerful connections

    M4 Max: four Thunderbolt 5 ports, two USB-A, HDMI, SDXC card slot, headphone jack. Supports up to five external displays. M3 Ultra: six Thunderbolt 5 ports, supports up to eight displays.

  • Pro apps fly

    Tens of thousands of AI-ready and pro apps including Logic Pro and Final Cut Pro run with mind-bending speed.
