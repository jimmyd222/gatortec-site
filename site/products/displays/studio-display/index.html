Studio Display

Studio Display. Brilliant in every way.

A do-it-all 27-inch 5K Retina display. 600 nits of sustained brightness, P3 wide color, True Tone. Plus a 12MP Center Stage camera, studio-quality three-mic array, and six-speaker sound with Spatial Audio.

Starting at $1,599

At a glance

Brilliant display. Great camera. Great sound.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • 5K Retina display

    14.7 million pixels at 218 ppi for stunning detail. 600 nits sustained brightness, P3 wide color, True Tone.

  • Center Stage camera

    A 12MP Ultra Wide front camera that automatically keeps you in frame as you move during video calls.

  • Studio-quality mics

    A three-mic array with directional beamforming captures clear voice and works great for music and video recording.

  • Six-speaker sound

    Four force-canceling woofers and two high-performance tweeters fill any room with rich sound — with Spatial Audio.

  • One-cable connection

    A single Thunderbolt cable charges your Mac at 96W and powers the display, camera, audio, and three additional USB-C ports.

  • Built-in Apple silicon

    An A13 Bionic chip powers the camera and audio features, including Center Stage and Spatial Audio.
