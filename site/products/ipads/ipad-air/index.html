Key features

What makes it special.

  • M3 chip

    A big performance jump from the previous generation — handles demanding apps, multitasking, and graphics with ease.

  • Two sizes — 11 or 13 inches

    Pick the size that fits how you work. Both feature gorgeous Liquid Retina displays with True Tone and P3 wide color.

  • Four bold colors

    Blue, Purple, Space Gray, and Starlight — make iPad Air your own.

  • Apple Pencil Pro support

    Get the latest Pencil features — squeeze, barrel roll, haptic feedback — for note-taking, drawing, and markup.

  • Apple Intelligence ready

    Built-in AI helps you write, summarize, generate, and stay on task — privately, on device.1

  • All-day battery life

    Up to 10 hours of web on Wi-Fi or watching video. Charges quickly over USB-C.
