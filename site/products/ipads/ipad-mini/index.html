iPad mini · A17 Pro chip

iPad mini. Mega power. Mini sized.

An ultra-portable iPad with the muscle of Apple Intelligence inside. The A17 Pro chip handles graphics-intensive apps and on-device AI in a form factor small enough to hold one-handed.1

Starting at $499

At a glance

Ultra-portable. Ultra-capable.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • A17 Pro chip

    Hardware-accelerated ray tracing, faster CPU and GPU — built to handle on-device Apple Intelligence and demanding apps.1

  • 8.3-inch Liquid Retina display

    Vibrant, sharp, and held one-handed. Wide color, True Tone, and great viewing angles.

  • Apple Pencil Pro support

    Squeeze for tool palettes, barrel roll for shaping, haptic feedback, Find My — full Pencil Pro features in the smallest iPad.

  • Apple Intelligence

    On-device AI helps you write, summarize, generate, and get things done — privately.1

  • Pocketable form factor

    Easy to carry in a jacket pocket, a small bag, or in hand. Perfect for field work, reading, and gaming.

  • 5G cellular option

    Stay connected anywhere. Wi-Fi + Cellular models support fast 5G.
