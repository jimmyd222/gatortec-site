iPad Pro. Unbelievably thin. Incredibly powerful.

The all-new M5 chip lifts iPad Pro performance to extraordinary new heights. Ultra Retina XDR display with tandem OLED for stunning visuals. Pair with Apple Pencil Pro and the Magic Keyboard for a complete mobile pro studio.1

Key features

What makes it special.

  • M5 chip — pro performance, anywhere

    The next leap in Apple silicon for iPad. CPU and GPU performance for demanding workflows like photo editing, 4K video, music production, and 3D modeling.

  • Ultra Retina XDR display

    Tandem OLED technology delivers extreme brightness, exceptional contrast, and pin-sharp detail. ProMotion for fluid scrolling and responsive interactions.

  • Apple Pencil Pro

    Squeeze for tool palettes, barrel roll for precise shaping, haptic feedback, and Find My support. The most advanced Apple Pencil ever.

  • Apple Intelligence built in

    On-device AI for writing, image creation, and natural language. Private by design.1

  • Thunderbolt 4 / USB-4

    Connect to high-performance accessories, displays, and external storage. Faster transfers, broader compatibility.

  • All-day battery life

    Up to 10 hours of surfing the web on Wi-Fi or watching video. Wi-Fi + Cellular models support fast 5G.
