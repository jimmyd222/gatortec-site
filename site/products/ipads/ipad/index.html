Key features

What makes it special.

  • A16 chip

    Fast and efficient — handles everything from web browsing and email to photo editing and casual games.

  • 11-inch Liquid Retina display

    A larger, more vibrant display with True Tone for comfortable reading any time of day.

  • Four cheerful colors

    Blue, Pink, Silver, and Yellow — pick the one that suits your style.

  • Apple Pencil support

    Compatible with Apple Pencil (USB-C) for note-taking, sketching, and markup — and with the original 1st-generation Apple Pencil.

  • Landscape camera

    A 12MP Ultra Wide front-facing camera positioned for landscape orientation — perfect for video calls.

  • All-day battery life

    Up to 10 hours of web browsing on Wi-Fi. Charges over USB-C.
