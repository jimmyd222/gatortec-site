MacBook Pro · M5, M5 Pro, M5 Max

MacBook Pro. Fast and furiously smart.

The 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pro brings next-generation speed and powerful on-device AI to personal, professional, and creative tasks. Featuring up to 24 hours of battery life1 and a stunning Liquid Retina XDR display.

Starting at $1,599

At a glance

Pro by design, pro by performance.

Key features

Pro performance, pro display, pro everything.

  • Supercharged by M5

    Along with its faster CPU and unified memory, M5 features a more powerful GPU with a Neural Accelerator built into each core, delivering faster AI performance. So you can blaze through demanding workloads at mind-bending speeds.

  • Step up to M5 Pro or M5 Max

    M5 Pro and M5 Max feature a next-generation CPU, faster unified memory, up to 2× faster SSD storage,2 and a more powerful GPU with a Neural Accelerator in each core — delivering pro-grade AI performance and on-device training capabilities.

  • Built for AI

    Apple silicon is designed to run demanding on-device AI workloads like LLM inference and training. And Apple Intelligence helps you write, express yourself, and get things done effortlessly with groundbreaking privacy protections at every step.3

  • Up to 24 hours of battery life1

    MacBook Pro delivers the same exceptional performance whether it's running on battery or plugged in.

  • Brilliant Liquid Retina XDR display6

    14.2-inch or 16.2-inch Liquid Retina XDR display with 1600 nits peak brightness,7 up to 1000 nits sustained brightness, and 1,000,000:1 contrast. Nano-texture option available to reduce reflections and glare.

  • Advanced camera and audio

    Stay perfectly framed and sound great with a 12MP Center Stage camera, three studio-quality mics, and six speakers with Spatial Audio and support for Dolby Atmos.

  • Connect it all

    Three Thunderbolt 5 ports (with M5 Pro/Max) or Thunderbolt 4 (with M5), a MagSafe 3 charging port, SDXC card slot, HDMI port, headphone jack, and Wi-Fi 7.8 Supports up to three external displays with M5 Pro, or up to four with M5 Max.

  • Privacy and security built right in

    Robust defenses against viruses and malware. FileVault encrypts your files so others cannot access them. Find My can help locate a lost or stolen Mac. Free software updates keep your Mac protected.
