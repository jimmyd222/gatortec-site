Apple Watch SE 3

Apple Watch SE 3. All the essentials. Less of the cost.

The most affordable Apple Watch — with the S10 chip, Crash Detection, Fall Detection, and activity tracking. A great first Apple Watch, a great pick for kids on Family Setup, and a great companion for everyday life.

Key features

What makes it special.

  • S10 chip

    Smooth, responsive performance — the same chip family as Series 11. Smarter Siri and faster app launches.

  • Crash and Fall Detection

    Apple Watch SE can detect a severe car crash or a hard fall and connect you to emergency services if you need help.1

  • Activity, Workout, and Sleep tracking

    Close your rings, track over 50 workout types, and get a nightly Sleep Score to better understand your sleep.

  • Always-on heart rate

    Heart rate monitoring with high and low heart rate notifications. Temperature sensing for cycle tracking.

  • Family Setup

    A great Apple Watch for kids or older family members without an iPhone — managed and paired from your iPhone.

  • Up to 18 hours of battery life

    All-day battery with low power mode extending up to 36 hours. Fast charging when you need it.
