Apple Watch Ultra 3

Apple Watch Ultra 3. Built for the outdoors.

The most rugged and capable Apple Watch ever. Aerospace-grade titanium case. Brightest display Apple has ever made on a Watch. 5G cellular. Up to 42 hours of battery (72 hours in Low Power Mode). Built for divers, climbers, runners, and explorers.1

Key features

What makes it special.

  • Titanium case, 49mm

    Aerospace-grade titanium is light, strong, and corrosion-resistant. Same case size in two finishes — Black or Natural Titanium.

  • Brightest Apple Watch display ever

    Up to 3000 nits of peak brightness. Always readable in direct sun. Wide-angle OLED for easy viewing from any angle.

  • Up to 42 hours of battery life

    All-day, two-day battery in typical use. Low Power Mode extends to 72 hours. Charges quickly when you need to top up.

  • 5G cellular and satellite

    Stay connected anywhere — calls, messages, music streaming — even when your iPhone isn't with you. Satellite connectivity for emergencies in remote areas.2

  • Action button

    A bright orange Action button you can program for your most-used workflow — start a workout, mark a waypoint, trigger a dive, or fire a Shortcut.

  • Recreational diving to 40m

    Apple Watch Ultra 3 works as a dive computer with compatible apps. Water-resistant to 100m. EN 13319 compliant.3

  • Advanced health sensing

    Hypertension notifications, sleep apnea notifications, ECG, Blood Oxygen,4 temperature sensing, and a nightly Sleep Score.

  • Precision dual-frequency GPS

    L1 + L5 dual-frequency GPS for the most accurate location data — even in cities or under tree cover.
