Apple Authorized Service Provider

Service done right. By Apple-certified techs.

GatorTec has been an Apple Authorized Service Provider since 2010. Real Apple parts, real Apple-certified technicians, real repair pricing — published below so you know what to expect. AppleCare+ coverage honored, in-warranty repairs free, and a real person on the phone if you have questions. Service is by appointment only — please call or message us before bringing in a device.

Mac iPhone iPad Apple Watch AirPods, Beats & Audio Apple TV & HomePod

1. Schedule a drop-off

Service is by appointment only — call or message us, and we'll schedule a time. Drop off at our Gainesville location, or ship from out of town. We diagnose, quote, and confirm before any work starts.

2. Genuine Apple parts

As an AASP, we use only genuine Apple parts. Your repair is performed by Apple-certified technicians using Apple-approved processes and diagnostics.

3. Real warranty

Apple-honored repairs come with a 90-day labor and parts warranty. We're here if anything comes up after you pick up.

Mac.

In-warranty Mac repairs are free. AppleCare+ covers accidental damage at fixed fees. Out-of-warranty repairs include a diagnostic fee that's applied to repair labor if you choose to proceed.

AppleCare+ saves you money.

AppleCare+ Service Pricing

Two incidents of accidental damage per year. Battery service when capacity drops below 80%. Apple-trained tech support 24/7. Repairs use genuine Apple parts and don't void your warranty.

Apple service programs

Free service programs and recalls.

Apple periodically runs free repair programs for known issues — many at no cost regardless of warranty status. If your device is affected, we'll honor the program.
