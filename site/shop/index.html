About Upgraded.

GatorTec was a seller on Upgraded's Apple device upgrade and financing marketplace. The marketplace is not currently available, and GatorTec is not currently selling on it.

If you have an existing Upgraded plan

Your plan and financing are managed by Upgraded and its lending partners — not by GatorTec. Your loan is held by either Citizens or Affirm, depending on when you enrolled. To find which lender holds your loan, check your monthly statements, payment emails, or the app you use to make payments.

Citizens

If your loan is held by Citizens, contact them through your Citizens account or use the contact information on your loan statements.

For questions about your Upgraded agreement or upgrade eligibility

Contact Upgraded directly through their help center, where you can search their knowledge base or start a live chat. You can also email their support team.

getupgraded.com/help · team@getupgraded.com

As a former seller, GatorTec is unable to access, service, or make changes to Upgraded accounts or financing.

Looking to buy a new Apple device outright?

GatorTec is an Apple Premier Partner and sells Apple products directly. We don't offer financing, monthly upgrade plans, trade-ins toward Upgraded plans, or migration of existing Upgraded accounts. For anything related to your Upgraded plan, please use the contact information above.
